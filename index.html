About Me

I am an assistant professor at the Swedish University of Agricultural Sciences (SLU) in Alnarp, with a background in plant breeding and genomics. My research focuses on genetic diversity, adaptation and improving environmental sustainability in crop plants.

Research

I involved in research projects involving genomic and phenomic studies on traits.I use approaches including GWAS, genomic selection, and machine learning to support crop improvement.
